Learning & Content Q: How do I start a lesson? A: Navigate to your desired subject using the menu or search bar, then tap on a lesson card to begin. Q: What subjects are available? A: The app currently supports Science, Geography, English, Math, and Chemistry, with more to come. Q: How do I add or submit flashcards or notes? A: Use the “Add flashcard” or “Notes” section available within each subject tab. Progress Tracking Q: Where can I view my progress? A: Visit the “Analysis” page or check the progress bars within each subject tile. Q: What does the percentage next to a subject mean? A: It shows your completion rate or performance in that subject. Notes & Collaboration Q: Can I take notes during lessons? A: Yes, head to the “Notes” section to write and save your thoughts or answers. Q: Can I share notes with others? A: This feature may be part of future releases. For now, notes are saved locally to your account. Leaderboard & Motivation Q: How does the leaderboard work? A: The leaderboard ranks users based on lesson completion and quiz performance. Q: How can I improve my rank? A: Focus on finishing lessons and scoring well on quizzes. Notifications & Updates Q: What are the “Updates” and “Missing” tabs for? A: They show new content uploads, score updates, and any missed or pending files/notes.
